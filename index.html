Welcome to La Mexicana!

La Mexicana Restaurant & Grocery, located in Newport, KY, offers a delicious array of authentic Mexican dishes. Customers rave about the flavorful tacos, margaritas, and homemade salsas. The menu features classics like Queso Dip, Guacamole, and Pozole soup. The atmosphere is welcoming and authentic, making it a popular spot for both locals and visitors. With a focus on traditional Mexican flavors and generous portions, La Mexicana is a go-to destination for those craving a true taste of Mexico. Don't miss out on their mouthwatering Barbacoa burrito and Carnitas tacos for a truly satisfying dining experience.
